    GUIDE

    Follow this guide to set up your account and start tracking your fitness journey.

    STEP 1

    Download & Install

    STEP 2

    Create Your Account

    Tap Sign Up and enter your email, name, and a secure password. Choose your fitness level

    STEP 3

    Explore the Dashboard

    Workout Plans – Personalized routines Progress Tracking – View stats, milestones Challenges & Community – Join group workouts and fitness challenges. Insights & Analytics – Get real-time feedback on your progress.

    STEP 4

    Start Your First Workout

    Go to the Workout Plans tab. Select a plan or create a custom workout. Follow guided exercises with video instructions. Log your sets, reps, and weights as you go.
